About

How I got where I'm going

Hello. I am a software developer living in the Upstate of SC. After graduating from Connecticut College, I decided to head south for a new adventure. I signed up for a year of nonprofit volunteering through AmeriCorps, packed my bags and moved to Spartanburg, SC. I had every intention of heading back North after completing my year of service but the southeast managed to capture a piece of my heart and I’ve been embracing my southern side ever since.

In the spring of 2016, while working as a strategy consultant for a nonprofit and community consulting form, a colleague and I set out to design a tool to make it easier for out clients to manage the systems that sustain their impact. This incredibily educational, challenging, and rewarding experience opened my eyes to the world of software development.

In the summer of 2017 I enrolled at The Iron Yard Greenville, a 12-week coding bootcamp, to jumpstart my software development experience in an immersive, fast-paced environment. I look forward to joining a team where I can help a company solve their largest problems with enthusiasm, passion and a sense of humor.

I'd love the chance to talk with you more about web and software development, dogs, and/or how shocked I am by the speed at which I started using (and loving) the phrase "y'all".
