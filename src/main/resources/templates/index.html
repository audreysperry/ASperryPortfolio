About Me

A bit of history.

Hello. I am a software developer living in the Upstate of SC. After graduating from Connecticut College, I decided to head south for a new adventure. I signed up for a year of nonprofit volunteering through AmeriCorps, packed my bags and moved to Spartanburg, SC. I had every intention of heading back North after completing my year of service but the southeast managed to capture a piece of my heart. I’ve been embracing my southern side ever since.

After working in the nonprofit sphere for 3 years I joined a nonprofit and community consulting firm in 2015 as a strategy consultant. In the spring of 2016, A colleague and I set out to design a tool to make it easier for out clients to manage the systems that sustain their impact. This incredibily educational, challenging, and rewarding experience opened my eyes to the world of software development.

In the summer of 2017 I enrolled at The Iron Yard Greenville, a 12-week coding bootcamp, to jumpstart my software development experience in an immersive, fast-paced environment. I am looking for an opportunity to help a company solve their largest problems with enthusiasm, passion and a sense of humor.

I'd love the chance to talk with you more about my work, my dog, and/or how shocked I am by the speed at which I started using (and loving) the phrase "y'all".
